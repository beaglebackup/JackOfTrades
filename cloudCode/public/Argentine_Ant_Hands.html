Argentine Ant Recommendations

Pest Proofing.

  • Sealing cracks and crevices and points of entry
  • Storage of attractive food items in airtight container
  • Creating sticky barriers with tangle-foot
  • Creating detergent water “moats”

Sanitation.

  • Removal of food debris by vacuum, moping or sponging from surface
  • Rinse cans, bottles or food container prior to disposal
  • Clean food traps from sinks
  • Dry wet surfaces
  • Separate organic from recyclables and landfill waste
  • Relocate trash receptacles away from building
  • Pick up fallen fruit
